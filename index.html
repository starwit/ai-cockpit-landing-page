Project Information

Demo environments linked on this page are part of a German research project and it was partly funded by the government of the federal republic of Germany. Project aims to keep humans in command and is organized by the Federal Ministry of Labour and Social Affairs. It is supposed to cover requirements from EU's AI act. More details can be found at https://www.kicockpit.eu.

Demo environments linked on this page are developed by Starwit Technologies GmbH and you can find more information at: https://starwit.de.

If you are interested in implementation & source code, please look at https://github.com/starwit/ai-cockpit-deployment . Pull requests and bug reports are always welcome!
